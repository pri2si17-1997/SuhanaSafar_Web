  • About Package

    About Package

    The Incredible India holiday package provides you an insight into the vivid traditions and lifestyle of the country. Exotic destinations of Rajasthan like Bikaner, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, Pushkar, Jaipur, Mandawa and Amber will leave you stunned with their architectural and cultural extravagance, whereas Delhi will leave an everlasting impact on your mind with its diversity. From the renowned religious centres of Khajuraho and Varanasi to the heritage town of Orchha, witness different colours of the country on the same palette. The historic cities of Agra and Fatehpur Sikri are sure to take you back into the golden era of Mughal reign.Package Summary: Day 1: Arrive Delhi Day 2: Delhi - Mandawa (280 Km, 6 Hours) Day 3: Mandawa - Bikaner (170 Km, 4 Hours) Day 4: Bikaner - Jaisalmer (323 Km, 6 Hours) Day 5: Jaisalmer Day 6: Jaisalmer - Jodhpur (319 Km, 6 Hours) Day 7: Jodhpur – Pushkar - Jaipur (330 Km, 6 Hours) Day 8: Jaipur Day 9: Jaipur - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra (285 Km, 6 Hours) Day 10: Agra Day 11: Agra - Jhansi - Orchha (By Train) Day 12: Orchha - Khajuraho (180 Km, 4 Hours) Day 13: Khajuraho – Sarnath - Varanasi Day 14: Varanasi – Delhi Day 15: Depart Delhi
