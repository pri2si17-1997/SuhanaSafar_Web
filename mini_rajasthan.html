  • About Package

    About Package

    The Mini Rajasthan holiday package provides you an insight into the religious and cultural heritage of the colourful North Indian state. Through ancient temples, magnificent forts, imposing palaces and dated museums, this tour will acquaint you with the architectural finesse of the bygone era. Be it City Palace of Jaipur, Goenka Double Haveli at Mandawa, Junagarh Fort in Bikaner or the Brahma Temple of Pushkar, every structure unfolds the glorious history of Rajasthan. The national capital of Delhi and contemporary capitals of several Muslim rulers - Agra and Fatehpur Sikri, will add to your enriching experience of exploring the best of India. Package Summary:
    Day 1: Arrive Delhi
    Day 2: Delhi – Mandawa (280 Km, 6 Hours)
    Day 3: Mandawa – Bikaner (170 Km, 4 Hours)
    Day 4: Bikaner
    Day 5: Bikaner – Pushkar (248 Km, 5 Hours)
    Day 6: Pushkar – Jaipur (145 Km, 3 Hours)
    Day 7: Jaipur
    Day 8: Jaipur – Fatehpur Sikri - Agra (285 Km, 5 Hours)
    Day 9: Agra – Depart Delhi (195 Km, 4 Hours)
