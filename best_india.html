  • About Package

    About Package

    The Best of India holiday package provides you a glimpse of all facets of India – from its rich history to its cosmopolitan culture. Unfold the magic of this beautiful land during a visit to the heritage sites of Delhi like Red Fort and Qutab Minar and the magnificent edifices of Jaipur like City Palace and Amber Fort. Agra will surely amaze you with its sparkling Taj Mahal and Fatehpur Sikri while Aurangabad holds surprises like Ajanta and Ellora Caves in store. Mumbai, with its tourist hotspots like Marine Drive and Gateway of India, marks a perfect end to the journey. Package Summary:
    Day 1: Arrive Delhi
    Day 2: Delhi
    Day 3: Delhi – Agra (205 Km, 5 Hours)
    Day 4: Agra – Fatehpur Sikri - Jaipur (235 Km, 6 hours)
    Day 5: Jaipur
    Day 6: Jaipur – Mumbai – Aurangabad (By Flight)
    Day 7: Aurangabad
    Day 8: Aurangabad – Mumbai (By Flight)
    Day 9: Mumbai
    Day 10: Depart Mumbai
