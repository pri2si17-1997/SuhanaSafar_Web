  • About Package

    About Package

    In the Golden Triangle holiday package, you get to experience different facets of India, including magnificent edifices and national landmarks, besides the warm hospitality of Indians. From the national capital Delhi, you will head to the royal city of Jaipur and thereafter, the city of Taj - Agra. Each city will astonish you with its attractions, such as Red Fort and India Gate in Delhi, Hawa Mahal and City Palace in Jaipur, Fatehpur Sikri and Taj Mahal in Agra. A few days in these cities would make you understand why the Golden Triangle is a prominent tourist attraction of India. Day Wise Itinerary: Day 1: Arrive Delhi Our representative will welcome you at airport/railway station with Floral garlands and will transfer you to your respective hotel. Assistance and check-in at hotel. Since you will be arriving after a long flight, rest of the time free to feel and acclimatized with this unique country with many cultures…called India. Overnight at hotel. Day 2: Delhi After breakfast, explore Delhi, visit Red Fort, Jama Mosque, Rajghat, later continue to New Delhi and also visit India Gate, President’s house & Qutab Minar. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight at hotel. Day 3: Delhi - Jaipur (265 Km, 6 Hours) After breakfast, drive to Jaipur, since car will be at your disposal, you can stop anywhere for a short stop. Assistance and check-in at hotel. Overnight at hotel. Day 4: Jaipur After breakfast, excursion to Amer, enjoy an elephant ride while going up to the hill, later visit City Palace, Observatory & Hawa Mahal. Afternoon free for shopping etc. Overnight at hotel. Day 5: Jaipur - Fatehpur Sikri – Agra (258 Km, 6 Hours) Breakfast at hotel, later drive to Agra, en-route visit deserted city Fathepur Sikri, here you will explore a massive and very impressive fort, abandoned due to shortage of water. Assistance and check in at hotel. Overnight at hotel. Day 6: Agra - Delhi (204 Km, 4 Hours) After breakfast, checkout from hotel today your excitement comes to an end, visit world famous monument of love “Taj Mahal” (closed on all Fridays), later also visit Agra fort. Afternoon drive to Delhi. Overnight at hotel. Day 7: Depart Delhi Transfer to airport in time to catch flight for onward destination.
